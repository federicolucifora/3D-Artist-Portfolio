Aletheia Photo Mosaic
Web Development | User Experience | Digital Interaction
Aletheia Photo Mosaic is a web browser-based digital experience created to engage fans of rap singer Izi in promoting his album “Aletheia”. Through the platform it will be possible, from a computer or smartphone, to take a picture through the webcam which will then be uploaded to an online database. The photos will then be used as tiles to generate a mosaic that will recreate the image of the album cover. People will be encouraged to upload new images so that they can see the mosaic in its entirety as soon as possible, and by accessing the “Watch the mosaic” section they will have the opportunity to observe the completion status of the mosaic at the time of navigation. Furthermore, it will be possible to zoom into the mosaic so as to be able to distinguish the single images that make it up.
