Ignorance = Fear, Silence = Death
2D Illustration | 2D Animation | Screenplay
"Keith Haring - Ignorance = Fear, Silence = Death" is an animated video that aims to give life to the homonymous work by Keith Haring. The figures present within it are represented in the short film through unprotected sexual intercourse, at the end of which the protagonist loses speech, hearing and sight. To tell the stor, the figures move inside the scene, physically going to the different places where he will lose his physical abilities. Even the video itself will participate in this disappearance: as the main character loses his speech, he will stop whistling. As he will lose his hearing, the video will lose its sound. Eventually, as he loses his sight, the image will turn completely black.
