Deposito Cinerario Italiano
Arduino | 3D Modeling | 3D Rendering | Space Design
When you think about death, it is easy to associate it with the concept of burial or cremation. However, this vision is a consequence of our society; there are many ways in which an individual might want to dispose of his body following his death. In Italy, however, the regulations in this regard are very stringent, so much so that some communities, first of all the Islamic one, do not have the possibility of following the burial methods provided for by their creed. The “Deposito Cinerario Italiano” was created with the aim of making people reflect on this issue; it’s a government agency that, within a dystopian scenario, imposes a single burial method on everyone: cremation. The ashes of each of the departed will be gathered within a single, large deposit, with the consequent loss of identity of the individual. Visitors of the installation will take on the role of operator of the Deposito Cinerario Italiano: through a control panel they will start the process of depositing the ashes, removing forever what the deceased had been in life.
