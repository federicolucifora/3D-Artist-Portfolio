About Me
I am a young designer who, as a child, fell madly in love with the videogames of the Pokémon and Mario franchises. Since then, my dream is to create videogames that inspire people as much as those two inspired me. As a communication designer, my goal is to combine functionality and aesthetics in what I do, since I believe both are essential for a good user experience.
